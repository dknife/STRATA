Summary

Introduces Laplacian Positional Encoding (LapPE) for graph transformers using eigenvectors of the graph Laplacian. Suffers from sign ambiguity (later addressed by SignNet). Requires symmetric matrices, making it inapplicable to directed graphs — a limitation STORM's reachability profiles do not have.
