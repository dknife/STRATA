Summary

A comprehensive collection of large-scale network datasets including social networks, citation networks, web graphs, and road networks. Reports effective diameters of 5-7 for social networks and 5-6 for citation networks, confirming the small-world property. STRATA uses the Facebook dataset from SNAP for real-world validation.
